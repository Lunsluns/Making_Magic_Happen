Testimonials from our Adoptors

Who are in love with their new best friend!.

This organization is wonderful. we are very new to the Rabbit world and they have been amazing at helping to answer all of our questions. I love that I can bring them to their adoption days and have their nails clipped. They not only gave us great recommendations for vets that work with bunnies (since our regular vet does not work with many exotic animals) but they also provided coupons to help discount the cost of the surgery. When we first brought our buns home I was constantly messaging Emily with the rescue asking about everything from enclosures to what snacks to feed them and even if certain behaviors where normal. we enjoy our buns so much and having this rescue to turn to for advice and to help answer our questions has been an amazing resource. the people at this rescue genuinely care about the animals and it goes past just adoption.

Jennifer Michel

June 25

They invited us into their bungalow and gave us all the time we needed to pick our next fur baby. We loved the experience so much we decided to foster our sweet baby Bella (aka Moore) from them and quickly became a foster fail. Thank you Magic Happens for all that you do and helping us in adopting our fur babies.

I can't say enough good things about MHRR! If you have any questions they can help, & are eager to help. Adopt don't shop, there are too many cuties needing homes at MHRR!

Not just about rabbits, but about the adoption process and how to educate the public about rabbits as well.
